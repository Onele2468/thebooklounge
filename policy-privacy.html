Privacy Policy

Who We Are

The Book Lounge is an independent bookstore located in Cape Town's CBD. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent relationship with our customers and website visitors.

Website: The Book Lounge | Contact: 71 Roeland Street, Cape Town

Data We Collect & Why

Comments & Communications

When you leave comments on our site, we collect your submitted data, IP address, and browser information to help detect spam.

Media & Uploads

If you upload images to our website, please avoid including embedded location data (EXIF GPS).

Contact Forms

Information submitted through our contact forms is used solely to respond to your inquiries.

Cookies & Tracking

  • Comment Cookies: Saves your name and email for up to 1 year for convenience.
  • Login Cookies: Temporary cookies to verify browser acceptance; login cookies last 2 days.
  • Session Cookies: "Remember Me" option persists login for 2 weeks.

Your Rights

You have the right to request an exported file of all personal data we hold about you, or request deletion of your data (excluding legally required records).
