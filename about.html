Our Story

The Book Lounge is an independent bookstore in the heart of Cape Town. We opened our doors in December 2007 and since then have become a hub for book lovers, writers, and thinkers alike.

We pride ourselves on our curated selection of books, ranging from the latest local fiction to international classics, children's literature, and non-fiction. Our staff are all passionate readers who love nothing more than helping you find your next favorite book.
