About Us

Discover the heart and story behind Book Lounge.

Our Story

The Book Lounge is an independent bookstore in the heart of Cape Town. We opened our doors in December 2007 and since then have become a hub for book lovers, writers, and thinkers alike.

We pride ourselves on our curated selection of books, ranging from the latest local fiction to international classics, children's literature, and non-fiction. Our staff are passionate readers who love helping customers find their next favourite book.
